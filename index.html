About Us

Strategic Edge is an AI marketing agency based in California. We empower local businesses with intelligent voice assistants and chatbots that capture and qualify leads even when you're busy on the job or after hours. Our mission is simple: help small businesses grow by ensuring every customer call or message receives a professional response and the lead is never lost.

What We Do

Voice AI Receptionist

Our AI voice agents answer inbound calls with natural, human‑like speech and can handle dozens of calls concurrently. They qualify leads, provide information and schedule appointments automatically, so you don’t have to pick up the phone to capture new business [3][5].

Chat Agents

Our chatbots offer 24/7 online support and respond instantly to website and social messages. Powered by advanced language understanding, they engage visitors, answer questions and guide potential clients through next steps. The bots learn from past interactions to deliver personalized, contextual responses that feel natural [1][6].
